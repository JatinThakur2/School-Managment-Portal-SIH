How the Website Works!!

  • Super Admin: He is the boss or head of all the admins who will add or edit or delete or view school, he can also add or update or delete or view block and district admins. He can also register and view the details about the teacher.
  • District Admin: He manages all the block admins and guide them. He can view school in his district. He can help school to resolve the query. Moreover, they can ask query too. He can send certain requests to super admin.
  • Block Admin: He is the head of all the school admin. He will keep an eye on their work. He can also view the schools in his particular block. Can ask and resolve school related query. He can request anything related to school to district admin.
  • School Admin/ Principal: He is the head of the particular school and has to takecare of each and every requirement of schools as he is answerable to all the admins above him. He assign the classes to teachers. He can view details and manage the data of school. He has right to view class details and teachers details. He can ask anything from the admins related to school.
  • Teacher: They are particular head of the class and play most important role in a school. They have rights in which they can view and add the result of students. They can update the personal information, marks, attendance, fees and any other details if required.
